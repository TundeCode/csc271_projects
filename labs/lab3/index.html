Jollof Rice

This is my favorite dish becuase its a cultural dish and my mom makes it best.
When ever I go to any type of nigerian party this dish is always present.
A fun fact about Jollof rice is theres a yearly contest on which african country can make the best jollof rice.

Ingredient Quantity Notes
Rice 3 cups Long-grain (parboiled preferred)
Tomatoes 4 large Fresh, blended
Tomato paste 2 tbsp For color and richness
Onions 2 medium 1 blended, 1 sliced
Red bell peppers 2 Blended with tomatoes
Scotch bonnet (chili) 1–2 Adjust for spice level
Vegetable oil 1/4 cup For frying sauce
Seasoning cubes 2 Maggi or Knorr
Bay leaves 2 Optional, for aroma
Chicken stock 2 cups Or water
Salt To taste Adjust as needed
